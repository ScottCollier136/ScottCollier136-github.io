Frequent Questions I Get Asked During Interviews

Do you prefer front-end, back-end, or full-stack?

I mostly have back-end experience, but I wouldn't go so far as to say I have a preferance. I'm willing to try anything.

Do you have experience working in teams and/or an agile environment?

Yes, I have worked in teams of up-to 6 active developers, in an agile environment.

Do you prefer a more casual or office-like environment?

From my experience both have their pros and cons. I don't specifically prefer one or the other, so long as the environment itself is friendly.

Have you ever taken a leadership role in a project?

When I worked at CIBC I took up a montoring role for a co-op student. When I worked for Autometrics I essentially managed a project by myself.

What would you say is your strength?

I have had to learn new technologies as I worked before, and think I am good at doing so.

What is a project you are proud of?

My work at Autometrics is easilly the most complex project I have done, so I am most proud of it.
